No one believed me, when I told them, what my roommate, did with the pizza.

I was 20, fresh out of home, and genuinely excited, for our Friday night, pizza tradition.

He seemed chill.

His aura, was completely off though, once the delivery driver, dropped off, our large pepperoni.

He grabbed, the middle.

Not one, not two, but all of the delicious, cheesy, middle pieces, were ruthlessly plundered, leaving only the crusts.

The AUDACITY, honestly, I was absolutely flabbergasted, by the sheer disrespect.

My jaw dropped.

He just stood there, smiling, munching away, on all the best parts, completely unbothered, by the absolute crime, he just committed.

My heart, felt BETRAYED; like my trust, had been shattered, into a million, tiny pieces.

I was cooked.

I was genuinely, locked in, trying to process, this level, of pure villainy.

He totally mogged it.

How could someone, truly, live with themselves, after such, a heinous act; I’m still reeling, from the absolute shock, and unfairness of it all.

Sometimes, I wonder, if they ever, regret their actions, or if no one, will ever believe me.
